Ranged Pumps adds a simple pump that extracts liquids within a configurable range.

The Pump is the only block added by the mod.

Usage

Place the Pump. It operates only without a redstone signal.

Redstone

A redstone signal disables the Pump until removed.

Energy

Uses Forge Energy by default (configurable to disable energy usage).

Internal tank

Holds 32 buckets by default (configurable). Stops when full.

Status

Right-click the Pump to view its current status.

Range & scanning

Pumps in a rectangular spiral, 1 block below the Pump. Scans up to 64 blocks in all horizontal directions (nearest-first), and from the Pump’s Y level down to bedrock.

Liquid replacement

Replaces pumped liquids with stone (can be disabled via config).

Output

Auto-pushes liquids to adjacent tanks. Pipes also work.

Chunkloading

The Pump is not chunkloaded. Use another mod for that.
